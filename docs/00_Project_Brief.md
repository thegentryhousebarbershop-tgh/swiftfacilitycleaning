# Swift Facility Cleaning

Version: 1.0

# Project Overview

## About The Company

Swift Facility Cleaning is a professional commercial cleaning company serving businesses throughout Missouri. The company specializes in reliable, high-quality facility maintenance for offices, medical facilities, schools, warehouses, gyms, government buildings, religious facilities, and other commercial properties.

Swift Facility Cleaning is not positioned as a budget cleaning service.

Instead, the company should be perceived as a dependable long-term facility maintenance partner that businesses trust to maintain clean, healthy, and professional environments.

The website should immediately communicate professionalism, reliability, cleanliness, responsiveness, and trust.

---

# Project Goal

The primary objective of this website is to generate qualified commercial cleaning leads.

Every page should encourage visitors to request a quote, schedule a consultation, or contact the company.

The website should function as the company's highest-performing digital sales representative.

The website should:

* Build credibility within seconds
* Clearly communicate services
* Demonstrate expertise
* Increase trust
* Improve local SEO rankings
* Convert visitors into leads
* Support future business growth

* Spacious sections
